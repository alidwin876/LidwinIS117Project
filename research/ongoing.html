Ongoing Research Projects

Current work focused on AI, data-driven modeling, and medical informatics. These projects involve building practical machine-learning systems, developing reproducible analytical workflows, and applying computational methods to real scientific and clinical problems.

LLM Analysis for Medical Image Captioning
Status: Active - Writing Publication

This project explores the capabilities of large language models (LLMs) in accurately generating descriptive captions for complex medical images (e.g., MRI and CT scans). The goal is to improve diagnostic efficiency through automated documentation.

  • Discipline: LLM Processing, Medical Informatics
  • Expected Completion: Winter 2025
Project Progress 80%
Development of VOC Chemical Smart Sensors
Status: Active - Prototype Testing

A multidisciplinary effort to design and test low-cost, high-sensitivity smart sensors for detecting Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) in environmental settings. This work aims to enhance real-time air quality monitoring systems.

  • Discipline: Electrical and Chemical Engineering
  • Expected Completion: Winter 2025
Project Progress 70%
