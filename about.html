About Me

Academic Foundation

I am an IT student concentrating on Management Information Systems (MIS), systems architecture, and applied medical AI. My work focuses on delivering efficient, secure technical solutions that bridge business strategy with technological implementation.

AI Research & Evaluation

My primary research area is the rigorous evaluation of Medical Image Captioning (MIC) systems. I work to establish transparent and reproducible metrics that improve the reliability and trustworthiness of AI models in clinical diagnostic pipelines.

Technical Methodology

I design and implement specialized PyTorch-based evaluation frameworks. I analyze vast medical imaging datasets, including MIMIC-CXR and CheXpert, to accurately benchmark clinical AI performance.

Operations Management

Strategic Career Path
