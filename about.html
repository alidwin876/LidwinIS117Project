About Me

Exploring the intersection of technology, research, and healthcare.

Academic Focus

Senior at NJIT in Information Technology with a concentration in Management Information Systems (MIS). My focus is on IT systems, project leadership, and medical AI.

Core Research Interest

My primary focus is on developing robust evaluation metrics for Medical Image Captioning (MIC). This work aims to improve the reliability and transparency of AI-generated radiology reports.

Research Methodology

Working under Dr. Fadi P. Deek, I develop reproducible Python and PyTorch pipelines. I rigorously evaluate models using large-scale medical datasets, including MIMIC-CXR and CheXpert.

Professional Experience

As Assistant Administrator at Bergen Community College, I manage large-scale testing operations. This role emphasizes compliance with ADA and FERPA standards.

Future Goals

I plan to pursue both an MBA and a PhD. My goal is to advance ethical, transparent, and impactful AI within the field of healthcare leadership.

Interests & Activities

My interests include tutoring youth in STEM subjects, cycling, hiking, and attending concerts. I also enjoy developing creative portfolio projects.
